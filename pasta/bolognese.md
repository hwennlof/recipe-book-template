# Lovely lovely meat sauce

## Ingredients

- 500 g beef mince
- 1-2 yellow onions
- 2-3 cloves of garlic
- 1 can of chopped tomatoes
- 1 beef stock cube
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

- Fry the beef in a pot until golden brown
- Chop and add onions and garlic, fry for a bit more
- Add tomatoes, and an equal amount of water
- Stir in stock cube
- Add salt and pepper
- Let simmer for at least an hour
